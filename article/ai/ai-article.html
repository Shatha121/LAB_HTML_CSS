What even Is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (Al) refers to technology that allows computers and machines to imitate human abilities - like learning, understanding, solving problems, making decisions, being creative, and even acting on their own. Basically, it's about teaching machines how to think and behave in ways that feel human.

How I'd Use AI as a Developer

I'd still write my code manually. I want to understand what I'm doing. But I'd use Al to help me learn - like asking for explanations, checking my logic, or exploring different ways to solve a problem. It's like having a super fast teammate who can teach you things without giving you the full answer.

My Personal Rules for Using AI

  1. Write code manually before asking AI .
  2. Ask AI only when stuck, not lazy .
  3. Always test and understand AI suggestions .
  4. Use AI as a tutor, not a crutch .
